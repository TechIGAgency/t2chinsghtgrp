Jean Joseph, Principal Data & AI Architect at Tech Insight Group LLC. With over 20 years of experience, I specialize in helping organizations unlock insights and improve customer engagement through advanced analytics, intelligent data pipelines, and generative AI solutions. My work spans designing scalable architectures, leading customer success initiatives, and speaking internationally on data-driven innovation.

While I bring deep technical expertise, my greatest impact comes through collaboration. At Tech Insight Group, I work alongside a talented network of professionals and partners who complement my skills in data engineering, visualization, and AI strategy. Together, we deliver scalable solutions that go beyond individual contributions—ensuring clients benefit from collective knowledge, diverse perspectives, and the strength of a team committed to operational excellence and long-term success.

He specializes in Microsoft Fabric, Copilot Studio, and Databricks, and is highly comfortable working with Generative AI, Agentic AI, Azure SQL Databases, Power BI, Azure AI Services, Microsoft Foundry, and Azure AI Search. His preferred scripting languages are SQL and Python, which he leverages to design scalable solutions and streamline complex workflows.

He is also a Microsoft Certified Trainer, international speaker, blogger, and Data & AI community builder. He has had the opportunity to speak at some of the most prestigious national and international conferences, including Microsoft Build & Visual Studio Live in the USA, Live! 360 Artificial Intelligence in Orlando USA, PASS Data Community Summit in the USA, SQLBits in London, Prague Data Conference in Europe, SQLConf in Germany, Databricks Summit in USA, Postgres Conference in the USA, and Microsoft Skill Up AI in the USA. In addition, Jean has contributed to countless SQL & Data Saturday events and actively engages with online user groups through platforms like Microsot Reactor, Meetup and Eventbrite, further extending his reach to global audiences.

Through his leadership in the Data Driven Community and initiatives like the Future Data Driven Summit, Jean connects executives, engineers, and learners by translating complex technical concepts into practical strategies. His mission is to adapt his communication style to speak to the widest possible audience—combining technical depth with strategic clarity—making him a trusted ally for organizations navigating the evolving landscape of AI and data engineering.

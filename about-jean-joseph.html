Jean Joseph Bio

About our Principal Technical Data & AI Architect

Jean Joseph is a Principal Technical Architect specializing in Data & AI at Tech-Insight-Group, and the founder of a global consulting and training firm focused on enterprise-grade solutions in cloud data, generative AI, and visualization. A Microsoft Certified Trainer and Copilot Champion, Jean empowers organizations through strategic architecture, hands-on demos, and enablement across Azure, Fabric, and OpenAI. With deep expertise in LLMs, Agentic AI, and vector databases, Jean leads with clarity, scalability, and impact.

As a dynamic speaker, prolific blogger, and passionate community builder, Jean has trained thousands through global conferences including Microsoft Build, Databricks events, PASS Data Community Summit, SQLBits, Microsoft Visual Studio Live, Live360, Prague, and PostgreSQL Conference. He mentors aspiring technologists and drives inclusive growth through the Data Driven Community, fostering innovation in data engineering, AI, and visualization. His mission: to make advanced tech accessible, reproducible, and transformative for teams everywhere.
